      Pirate King Of Another World : The Forgotten One

      Release date: October 20, 2024

      ActionAnimeSurvival

      Hatake is a young unemployed man whose life has always been filled with self-doubt. Since childhood, he has been mocked and belittled by his family, friends, and even strangers around him. His life is plagued with failure and rejection, until one day, in despair, he decides to take a walk in the city park to escape the pressures of life.

      Unknown Corporation : Secret Mission #S2

      Release date: October 21, 2024

      ActionAnimeSurvival

      This story follows Yazid and his team Agi, Alfi, Malik, Nanda, and Aldy tasked with investigating an online gambling and drug syndicate abroad. After capturing the syndicate leader, they face a brutal retaliatory attack. Through teamwork and strategy, the team successfully dismantles the syndicate, realizing that new threats may always emerge. Though they return victorious, they remain vigilant for future missions.

      Kodoku : Living In The Cursed City

      Release date: October 26, 2024

      ActionAnimeHorror

      Nagato, a young man, has just moved to a cursed city. He lives alone in this cursed place. Strange things happen every day in the city, leaving Nagato feeling confused and depressed as he witnesses the bizarre events day after day.

      Jukoku

      Release date: October 31, 2024

      ActionAnimeHorror

      "Jukoku" is a horror anime film that follows the story of Mizuki, a young man trapped in a cursed city. Once a peaceful and lively town, the city is now shrouded in an oppressive darkness. People have begun to experience strange events and disappear one by one, leaving only faint whispers and traces of blood in the dark corners of the streets.
